Chicken Shawarma Marinade

How to make a Turkish Style Shawarma Marinade

A delicious recipe I found on youtube, created by The Golden Balance channel. I have personally tried and it really is amazing! You can find the full recipe here.

Ingredients:

  • 680g chicken thighs
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 30g hot pepper paste
  • 61g yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1g dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1g ground cumin
  • 1g paprika
  • 3g kosher salt
  • 1g ground black pepper
  • 30ml olive oil
