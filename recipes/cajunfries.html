Cajun Fries

Battered Cajun Fries recipe!

Cajun fried dishes are a spicy Louisiana specialty, featuring proteins coated in Cajun seasoning and fried to crispy perfection. With bold flavors of paprika, garlic, and cayenne, each bite offers a tantalizing combination of crunch and spice, making it a deliciously satisfying treat.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 large russet potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp vinegar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 2-3 cups frying oil
  • 1-2 Tbsp cajun seasoning

Steps:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin fries. Soak them in cold water with vinegar for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  3. In a bowl, mix the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Coat the fries in the flour mixture and shake off the excess.
  5. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (180°C).
  6. Fry the potatoes in batches for 5-6 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Drain the fries on a paper towel-lined plate and sprinkle with cajun seasoning.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
