Ice Tea

Let's make a refreshing lemon ice tea

Lemon iced tea is a refreshing beverage that combines the briskness of black tea with the tangy zest of lemon. It's typically brewed with tea leaves, water, and lemon juice, then chilled and served over ice, making it perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 gallon boiling water
  • 6 black tea bags
  • 1 Â½ cups white sugar
  • 4 limes or lemons, juiced

Steps:

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a large pot. Remove from heat, and add the tea bags. Let steep for about 5 minutes.
  2. Remove the tea bags, and stir in the sugar until dissolved. Add the lemon juice, and pour into a large pitcher.
  3. Refrigerate until cold, then serve over ice.
